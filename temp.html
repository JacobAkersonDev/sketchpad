Welcome to the Division of Inclusive Excellence's (IE) Consultation & Support Request Form! This request form is designed to be used by entire campus community as we work in partnership to embed successful, just, and equitable practices into the fabric of our campus.

Use this tool when your campus group, unit, or office requires a consultation or support for needs such as:

  • Guidance to address equity dilemmas
  • Inclusive Excellence learning opportunities
  • Diversity, equity, inclusion, belonging, and justice (DEIBJ) toolkits
  • Support to develop/research frameworks to address issues of diversity, inclusion, and belonging
  • Assistance with assessing current efforts to implement AICAP goals in your area
  • DEIBJ marketing and communication support
  • Keynote presentations
  • Logistical support for student-focused, equity programming in support of student belonging and success

Thank you for your desire to partner with IE to help our campus fulfill the Antiracist Inclusive Campus Action Plan (AICAP) goals. Together we can hold the University and ourselves accountable for making equity-centered decisions and policies, as we work to improve our campus.

To make the best selection for your consultation or support request, please refer to the IE Offices tab to learn more about the work of each of our offices.

There are four offices in the Division of Inclusive Excellence. Office for Cultural Transformation (OCT) OCT is the home of the Belonging Education & Support System (BESS) which works to create an inclusive space of belonging education and manages reporting and addressing incidents of bias on the Sac State campus.

The Faculty Diversity & Inclusion (FDI) is also a part of OCT and is dedicated to centering and promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in the recruitment, development, and retention of teaching faculty and college administrators.

Inclusive Excellence Learning (IEL) works with strategic campus and community partners to design and provide learning activities to expand diversity competency at Sacramento State.

Office for Disability Justice (ODJ)

ODJ provides education and guidance on disability-related issues, and actively confronts ableism to bring change at the systemic level and create a more inclusive, welcoming campus culture for Sac State's students, staff, faculty, and visitors.

Office for Equal Opportunity (OEO)

OEO offers training and outreach to campus constituents on discrimination, harassment, or retaliation. In addition to training and consultations, OEO fosters adherence to University policies on sexual misconduct, sexual exploitation, dating or domestic violence, and stalking.

Office of the Vice President & University Diversity Office (OVP)

The OVP consists of Vice President, Communication & Programs Specialist, and Director of Operations. The OVP team works together to inspire strategic campus partnerships with students, organizations, and campus units to lead the campus' efforts to transform the campus into an antiracism, equity-centered, inclusive, and healing university.

IE Consultation & Support Request Guidelines

To proceed to request form, please read the guidelines that follow carefully.

  • The IE Consultation & Support Request form is to be used by Sac State students, staff, faculty, and administrators to request support to educate and empower members of the Hornet community to address DEIBJ concerns.
  • Making a request should serve as a starting point and check-in resource on the journey of transforming your organization, team, unit, or office to become a space of healing and inclusivity.
  • We operate using consultative model that allows us to assess the request and set an intake meeting. We work in partnership with you to provide the most fitting support or set of recommendations to support your request.
  • At times, the requested support may fall outside of the scope of our expertise or focus, and we may guide you to see support from another, more appropriate, resource on campus.
  • When your ask is within our scope, you will be contacted by our team for to begin the consultative process to determine how best to fulfill your request.
  • Requests for support logistics, assessment, communication, keynote presentations, workshops, and other services are considered complete upon receipt of the deliverable.
  • The result of consultation requests are typically robust recommendations developed from our assessment and are created in partnership with you. We offer support, guidance, and suggestions on best practices, but we do not implement recommendations for you.
  • All requests require a follow-up IE Consultation & Support Summary Request Evaluation to be completed a link to which will be sent via a summary email.
  • The IE Consultation & Support Summary Request Evaluation must be completed within the designated time frame. Incomplete or missing evaluations may prohibit timely processing and consideration of future consultation and support requests.
